Seven Types of Contrast

An Introduction to Color Theory

Contrast of Hue

Created by the use of two distinct hues.

  • — RGB 158, 175, 127
  • — RGB 89, 97, 140

Contrast of Value

Use of a lighter color combined with a darker color.

  • — RGB 255, 255, 255
  • — RGB 255, 255, 255

Contrast of Temperature

The contrast between cool (blue, green, purple) and warm (red, orange, yellow) colors.

  • — RGB 255, 255, 255
  • — RGB 255, 255, 255

Contrast of Complement

Manifested through the combination of colors opposite one another on the color wheel.

  • — RGB 255, 255, 255
  • — RGB 255, 255, 255

Simultaneous Contrast

A contrast between nearly but not quite complementary colors.

  • — RGB 255, 255, 255
  • — RGB 255, 255, 255

Contrast of Saturation

The difference between a pure and a washed out color.

  • — RGB 255, 255, 255
  • — RGB 255, 255, 255

Contrast of Proportion

Contrast achieved by difference of relative proportion.
